This is a famous pong game. Pong is a table tennis themed twitch arcade sports video game, featuring simple two-dimensional graphics. The game can be played by two players, from opposite ends. Player must hit the ball with their paddle, and if they fail to do so, their opponent will receive the point. On top is a score title that indicates the scores of both players.

main.py file has the behaviour of the game.
ball.py file defines the properties and behaviour of the ball.
paddle.py file contains the properties and behaviour of the paddle.
score.py is responsible for tracking the score of each player and updating it.
